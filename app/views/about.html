Best of Bot Beta

Best of Bot is an IRC bot/website that tracks the best moments of Daily Tech News Show, an (aptly named) daily tech news podcast hosted by Tom Merritt. During the Christmas break, Tom does a "best of" show with clips from the previous year. This is obviously a lot of work, so the Best of Bot tracks potential best of moments to help out. To record a best of moment, join the chat room at irc.dtns.tv, and use the !b identifier (or bangtag, as I'm calling it) followed by a short description. This will then calculate the timestamp in the YouTube video and save it.
